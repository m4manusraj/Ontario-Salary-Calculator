Tax Rates and Contributions

Federal Tax Rates:

- 15% on the portion of taxable income that is $49,020 or less

- 20.5% on the portion of taxable income over $49,020 up to $98,040

- 26% on the portion of taxable income over $98,040 up to $150,000

- 33% on any taxable income exceeding $150,000

Ontario Tax Rates:

- 5.06% on the portion of taxable income that is $47,937 or less

- 7.7% on the portion of taxable income over $47,937 up to $95,875

- 10.5% on the portion of taxable income over $95,875 up to $110,076

- 12.29% on the portion of taxable income over $110,076 up to $150,000

- 13.16% between $150,000 and $220,000

- 13.16% + High-income Surtax on any taxable income exceeding $220,000 (the surtax increases the effective tax rate to 20.53%)

CPP (Canada Pension Plan) Contributions:

- Employee Contribution Rate: 5.95% on earnings up to $68,500

- Employee Contribution Rate: 4.00% on earnings between $68,500 and $73,200 (for 2024)

- Employer Contribution Rate: Matches employee contributions for both tiers

Employment Insurance (EI) Premiums:

- Employee Contribution Rate: 1.66% on earnings up to $63,200

- Employer Contribution Rate: Approximately 2.326% (1.4 times the employee contribution rate)
